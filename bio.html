My High School

AOIT

While in AOIT, I was introduced to the high tech industry through various hands
on experience and courses, such as introductory media art. I was amazed by how
much I could do with just a screen, a keyboard, and a mouse. I immediately fell
in love with it. In senior year, my love and passion for technologies inspired
me to enroll in a computer programming class, where I learned basic javascript
coding techniques. Ever since then, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in
computer science.

My City College

Basic programming

In college, I have exposed myself to a wide range of courses related to computer
science and programming, especially in C++ and Java. There, I created workpieces
such as simple graphics that involve temperature conversion, which disapplies a
screen that can convert between celsius to fahrenheit. I further applied the knowledge
I obtained from classes by spending my spare time to code a small game with a group of
friends who share the same interests and hobbies. It was more difficult than we
thought, because there are many components that must go together perfectly for
the game to run smoothly and efficiently.

My UCSD

Know the problem

When we encounter a challenge, we would discuss with each other to find a
solution to the problem.This allowed me and the group to share ideas that
would improve every aspect of the game, as well as our understanding in the
secret of coding. With the extensive in-class experience and the extracurricular
activity in which I am involved in, I have made adequate preparation for upper

Overall

Creatively

Finding an alternative solution allows me to learn what my mistakes could be, as well as
providing me an opportunity to try out new things. It also encourages me to look at the
