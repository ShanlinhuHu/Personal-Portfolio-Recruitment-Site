Learning Technologies Consultant(1.5 years)

I often see students browsing the internet for inaccurate online resources because
they cannot find tutoring sessions for their classes or timeslots that work for
their schedules. I understand how it feels to struggle through classes in the middle
of the semester, so I took the initiative to offer assistance.
